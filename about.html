Park Sungjin

  • Park Sungjin, or in Korean, 박성진 (朴晟鎭), is a South Korean singer and guitarist
  • Also known as SUNGJIN
  • He is the leader of South Korean band DAY6
  • He was born on January 16, 1993
  • Speaks with a strong Busan satoori (accent)
  • Widely-known for his raspy voice
  • He used to play cajon and djembe before playing the guitar
  • He has an older sister, and is the youngest in the family
  • He was a radio host for Station Z on every Wednesday night from November 2022 to June 2023
  • The session name was Sungjin's D-DAY 성진의 D-DAY
  • The experience inspired to the creation of the sixth track from his solo debut album,
    "Wednesday Night"
